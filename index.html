About Me

Hi, I'm Donovan Butcher, a web developer based in South Salt Lake who's passionate about helping small businesses establish a professional online presence.

I started learning web development in March 2026 through Frontend Simplified, where I've been building a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. What I enjoy most is seeing a website come together into something that clearly represents a person, a business, or a brand. There's something satisfying about taking an idea and turning it into a clean, thoughtful design that people can be proud to share.

Right now, I'm focused on working with small businesses that are ready for their first website. As I continue growing my portfolio, I'm excited to partner with businesses that have a unique brand and a story to tell. Every project is an opportunity to improve my craft while creating something that genuinely helps my clients.

Outside of web development, I spend my time on things like dancing, playing guitar, or generally taking care of my mind and body. I try to approach both life and development with a simple mindset: you won't know how to do it until you've done it. That philosophy keeps me curious, willing to learn, and always looking for ways to improve.
